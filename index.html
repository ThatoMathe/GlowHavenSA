Meet One Mathe

Your trusted beauty therapist with world-class expertise

Creating Beauty, Building Confidence

Born and bred in Mahikeng, North West, One Mathe is a passionate beauty therapist who went to Mmabatho High School before honing her craft at Beautiko College. With a year of formal training and another year of hands-on experience in the beauty industry, One has mastered a wide range of services including professional makeup artistry, luxurious facials, meticulous manicures and pedicures, precise waxing, brow tinting, eyelash installations, and flawless wig applications.

Her career highlights include working aboard cruise ships and in premium spas, where she refined her skills in delivering world-class beauty services. Clients adore One for her sweet, kind demeanor and her exceptional ability to create a relaxing, luxurious environment where everyone feels pampered and beautiful.

One's dream for Glow Haven is to grow into a mobile salon, bringing premium beauty services directly to clients across the region. She takes immense pride in helping people feel confident in their skin, offering affordable luxury that makes every client feel special.
